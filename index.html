Le Cadran Africa

We are a software company focused on providing solutions for call centers and debt collection, bulk SMS services, and cybersecurity. Our AI-driven software is built to boost efficiency, optimize operations, and deliver customized solutions for call centers. Our platform simplifies call management by offering features such as inbound and outbound calling, call conferencing, forwarding, queuing, interactive voice menus, and intelligent call routing, ensuring smooth communication and enhanced customer satisfaction. With real-time performance metrics, you can track agent productivity, call volume, response times, and customer interactions, giving you valuable insights to improve efficiency and service quality.

  • Inbound and outbound calling
  • Call conferencing
  • Call forwarding and queuing
  • Voice recording and agent management
  • Detailed performance and statistical metrics
  • Automations
